Course Structure

Each topic in this course is organized into three core components to enhance understanding and retention:

  1. Reading Material

    • Comprehensive texts, articles, and guides to build a solid theoretical foundation.

    • Presents key concepts, examples, and detailed explanations for a thorough understanding.

  2. Video Material

    • Engaging video lectures, tutorials, and demonstrations to visually convey concepts.

    • Complements the reading material and supports auditory and visual learners.

  3. Multiple Choice Questions

    • Interactive quizzes to evaluate understanding and reinforce knowledge.

    • Includes detailed feedback to clarify misconceptions and strengthen learning outcomes.

This structured approach ensures a balanced integration of theory, practical application, and self-assessment for effective learning.
