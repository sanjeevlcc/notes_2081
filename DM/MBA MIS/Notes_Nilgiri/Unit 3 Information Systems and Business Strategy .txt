INTRODUCTION TO INFORMATION SYSTEMS AND BUSINESS STRATEGY
==================================================================

      . Business strategy means the long term plan of an organization to
        achieve its goals
      
      . It explains how a business will compete in the market
      
      . It helps decide what products services customers markets and resources
        the organization will focus on
      
      . Information System strategy means the plan for using information
        technology and digital systems to support business strategy
      
      . In modern organizations business strategy and IS strategy are closely
        connected
      
      . A company cannot compete effectively without proper use of information
        systems
      
      . Information Systems help organizations collect data process data make
        decisions improve operations serve customers and create competitive
        advantage


==================================================================
EXAMPLE
==================================================================

      . A retail company wants to become the fastest grocery delivery company
        in a city
      
      . Its business strategy is fast delivery and customer convenience
      
      . Its Information System strategy may include
      
              . Online ordering system
            
              . Mobile application
            
              . Real time inventory system
            
              . GPS based delivery tracking
            
              . Customer database
            
              . Digital payment system
            
              . Business Intelligence dashboard
      
      . Here Information Systems directly support business strategy

3.1 ROLE OF IS IN BUSINESS STRATEGY
==================================================================

        . Information Systems play an important role in business strategy
        
        . They help organizations plan control coordinate and compete
        
        . They support daily operations as well as long term strategic decisions
        
        . IS helps management understand what is happening inside and outside
          the organization
        
        . IS converts raw data into useful information for decision making




------------------------------------------------------------------
MAJOR ROLES OF IS IN BUSINESS STRATEGY
------------------------------------------------------------------


1  Supporting Operational Efficiency

          . IS helps automate routine business activities
          
          . It reduces manual work
          
          . It saves time and cost
          
          . It improves accuracy




          Examples
          
          . Billing system in supermarket
          
          . ATM transaction system in bank
          
          . Online ticket booking system
          
          . Inventory management system




2  Supporting Better Decision Making

          . IS provides timely accurate and relevant information
          
          . Managers can make decisions based on data rather than guesswork



          Examples
          
          . Sales dashboard helps identify best selling products
          
          . Attendance system helps identify irregular students
          
          . Bank MIS helps identify loan default risk
          

3  Improving Customer Service

          . IS helps businesses serve customers faster and better



          Examples
          
          . CRM system records customer complaints and service history
          
          . Online tracking system helps customers track delivery
          
          . Chatbot answers customer queries quickly
          




4  Supporting Innovation
          
          . IS helps create new products services and business models



          Examples
          
          . Mobile banking
          
          . Ride sharing apps
          
          . Online food delivery
          
          . E commerce marketplace
          
          . Digital wallet
          



5  Supporting Cost Reduction

          . IS reduces paperwork manual processing and duplication of work



          Examples
          
          . Online form submission reduces printing cost
          
          . Automated payroll reduces manual calculation
          
          . ERP reduces repeated data entry






6  Supporting Competitive Advantage

          . IS helps organizations perform better than competitors



          Examples
          
          . Faster delivery
          
          . Personalized recommendation
          
          . Lower cost
          
          . Better customer experience
          
          . Data driven decision making

INFORMATION SYSTEMS AT DIFFERENT MANAGEMENT LEVELS
==================================================================

Operational Level

      . Uses TPS
      
      . Records daily transactions
      
              Examples
              
              . Sales entry
              
              . Bill payment
              
              . Attendance entry
              
              . Stock update


Middle Management Level

      . Uses MIS
      
      . Generates reports and summaries
      
              Examples
              
              . Monthly sales report
              
              . Branch wise performance report
              
              . Complaint resolution report




Decision Making Level

      . Uses DSS
      
      . Supports semi structured decisions
      
      Examples
              
              . What if sales increase by 20 percent
              
              . Which product should be promoted
              
              . Which branch should get more inventory





Top Management Level

        . Uses ESS
        
        . Supports strategic decisions
        
        Examples
                
                . Market expansion
                
                . Investment planning
                
                . Long term growth strategy
                
                . Competitor analysis

3.2 STRATEGIC ALIGNMENT OF IS AND BUSINESS GOALS
==================================================================
        
        . Strategic alignment means matching Information System strategy with
          business goals
        
        . IS should not be developed randomly
        
        . It should support the main objectives of the organization
        
        . If business goal and IS strategy are not aligned technology investment
          may fail
        
        . Alignment ensures that technology creates business value
        

        ------------------------------------------------------------------
        EXAMPLE OF STRATEGIC ALIGNMENT
        ------------------------------------------------------------------
        
        Business Goal
        
        . Increase customer satisfaction



        IS Strategy
        
        . Implement CRM system
        
        . Create customer complaint dashboard
        
        . Use chatbot for quick response
        
        . Send service feedback form



        Result
        
        . Faster complaint handling
        
        . Better customer relationship
        
        . Improved customer loyalty




------------------------------------------------------------------
ANOTHER EXAMPLE
------------------------------------------------------------------
        
        Business Goal
        
        . Reduce inventory cost
        
        
        
        IS Strategy
        
        . Implement inventory management system
        
        . Use barcode or QR code scanning
        
        . Connect sales system with stock system
        
        . Generate reorder alerts
        
        
        
        
        Result
        
        . Less overstock
        
        . Less stock shortage
        
        . Better working capital management


==================================================================
WHY STRATEGIC ALIGNMENT IS IMPORTANT
==================================================================

        . It ensures technology supports business priorities
        
        . It reduces wasteful IT investment
        
        . It improves management decision making
        
        . It improves coordination between departments
        
        . It helps achieve competitive advantage
        
        . It improves customer service
        
        . It supports long term growth
        
        . It improves organizational performance
        




==================================================================
PROCESS OF STRATEGIC ALIGNMENT
==================================================================
          
          1  Understand Business Goals
          
                  . Identify what the organization wants to achieve
                  
                  Examples
                  
                          . Increase sales
                          
                          . Reduce cost
                          
                          . Improve customer service
                          
                          . Expand market
          
          
          2  Identify Information Needs
          
                  . Find what data and information are needed to achieve the goals
                  
                  Examples
                  
                          . Customer data
                          
                          . Sales data
                          
                          . Cost data
                          
                          . Inventory data
                          
                          . Competitor data
                  
          
          3  Select Suitable Information Systems
          
                    . Choose systems that support the business goal
                    
                    Examples
                            
                            . CRM
                            
                            . ERP
                            
                            . MIS
                            
                            . DSS
                            
                            . E commerce system
                            
                            . BI dashboard
                            
          
          4  Integrate Systems with Business Process
          
                    . IS should be connected with daily work process
                    
                    . Employees should be trained to use the system
                    
                    . Data should flow between departments
          
          
          5  Monitor and Improve
          
                    . Management should continuously review system performance
                    
                    . Weaknesses should be corrected
                    
                    . System should be upgraded when business needs change

3.3 COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE THROUGH IS
==================================================================

        . Competitive advantage means the ability of a business to perform better
          than competitors
        
        . Information Systems can help organizations create competitive advantage
        
        . IS can improve speed cost quality innovation and customer service
        
        . In digital economy many companies compete mainly through information
          systems




------------------------------------------------------------------
WAYS IS CREATES COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE
------------------------------------------------------------------


1  Cost Leadership

        . IS helps reduce operating cost
        
        . Automation reduces labor and paperwork
        
        . Better data reduces waste
        
        Example
        
              . Online banking reduces need for many physical branch services
              
              . ERP reduces repeated work and improves resource use






2  Differentiation

      . IS helps provide unique services that competitors do not offer
      
      Example
      
            . Mobile app based service tracking
            
            . Personalized product recommendation
            
            . One click ordering
            
            . AI based customer support
            





3  Customer Focus
      
      . IS helps understand customer needs better
      
      Example
      
            . CRM stores customer history
            
            . BI analyzes customer behavior
            
            . Loyalty system tracks repeat customers
            




4  Speed and Flexibility

        . IS helps respond quickly to market changes
        
        Example
        
                . E commerce company changes price instantly
                
                . Online food delivery tracks demand in real time
                
                . Retail company updates stock automatically






5  Innovation in Business Model

        . IS helps create new ways of doing business
        
        Example
        
                . Airbnb connects hosts and guests without owning hotels
                
                . Uber connects drivers and riders without owning taxis
                
                . Daraz connects sellers and buyers through online marketplace
                





6  Better Supply Chain Management

        . IS connects suppliers manufacturers distributors and customers
        
        Example
        
                . Retail company automatically orders stock when inventory is low
                
                . Logistics company tracks goods in real time

==================================================================
PORTER’S FIVE FORCES MODEL
==================================================================

        https://hrcoursesonline.com/porters-five-forces-model/

      
      
      . Porter’s Five Forces model is used to analyze industry competition
      
      . It helps organizations understand the competitive environment
      
      . Information Systems can be used to reduce threats and strengthen
        competitive position
      

------------------------------------------------------------------
1  Threat of New Entrants
------------------------------------------------------------------

    . New companies may enter the market and increase competition
    
    . IS can create entry barriers
    
    
    
    
    How IS Helps
    
            . Strong digital platform
            
            . Large customer database
            
            . Loyalty program
            
            . Efficient supply chain
            
            . High switching cost
            

      Example
      
          . A bank with strong mobile banking and customer database is harder to
            compete against




------------------------------------------------------------------
2  Bargaining Power of Suppliers
------------------------------------------------------------------
      
      . Suppliers may increase price or reduce quality
      
      . IS helps manage supplier relationship
      
      
      
      How IS Helps
      
          . Supplier management system
          
          . E procurement
          
          . Supplier performance dashboard
          
          . Multiple supplier database
          
          . Real time stock monitoring
          
      
      Example
      
      . A retail company can compare supplier price quality and delivery time
        using MIS


------------------------------------------------------------------
3  Bargaining Power of Customers
------------------------------------------------------------------

. Customers can demand lower price better service and higher quality

. IS helps improve customer satisfaction and loyalty

      How IS Helps
      
            . CRM system
            
            . Personalized offers
            
            . Customer support system
            
            . Feedback system
            
            . Loyalty program
      
      
      Example
      
            . E commerce platform recommends products based on customer behavior
      

------------------------------------------------------------------
4  Threat of Substitute Products or Services
------------------------------------------------------------------

. Substitute products can replace existing products

. IS helps organizations innovate and provide better service



        How IS Helps
        
                . Digital product innovation
                
                . Mobile app service
                
                . Online support
                
                . Product customization
                
                . Subscription model
        
        
        Example
        
              . Online streaming services became substitute for traditional TV


------------------------------------------------------------------
5  Rivalry Among Existing Competitors
------------------------------------------------------------------

. Competition among existing firms may be high

. IS helps improve efficiency service quality and speed

How IS Helps

        . Business Intelligence
        
        . Pricing analytics
        
        . Digital marketing
        
        . Customer analytics
        
        . Faster service delivery
        

Example

      . Telecom companies use data analytics to reduce customer churn

------------------------------------------------------------------
CASE STUDY 1  AIRBNB AND DIGITAL PLATFORM STRATEGY
------------------------------------------------------------------

. Airbnb is a peer to peer accommodation platform

. It connects hosts and guests through a digital marketplace

. Airbnb does not own hotels like traditional hotel companies

. Its main strength is its information system platform

. The platform manages listings bookings reviews payments host profiles
  and guest information




IS Strategy

      . Build trust between strangers
      
      . Use review and rating system
      
      . Use host and guest profiles
      
      . Use Superhost certification
      
      . Use data to improve platform quality

Business Strategy

        . Offer accommodation without owning properties
        
        . Expand globally through digital platform
        
        . Provide alternative to hotels
        
        . Create trust and convenience for users



Competitive Advantage

        . Low physical asset cost
        
        . Large global network
        
        . User generated listings
        
        . Trust indicators
        
        . Data driven platform governance


MIS Lesson

      . Digital platforms can create new business models
      
      . Trust data and system design are strategic assets

SIMPLE DATA INTERPRETATION EXAMPLE FOR IS STRATEGY
==================================================================

Branch        Monthly Customers     Online Users     Complaints

Butwal             5000                3200              150

Pokhara            4200                2600              120

Kathmandu          8000                6200              300

Chitwan            3500                1800               90



Analysis

        . Kathmandu has highest number of customers and online users
        
        . Kathmandu also has highest number of complaints
        
        . Butwal has good online adoption but still has many complaints
        
        . Chitwan has lower online users compared to total customers



Decision

        . Improve customer support in Kathmandu
        
        . Promote digital service awareness in Chitwan
        
        . Use complaint analytics to improve service quality
        
        . Use MIS dashboard to monitor branch performance
