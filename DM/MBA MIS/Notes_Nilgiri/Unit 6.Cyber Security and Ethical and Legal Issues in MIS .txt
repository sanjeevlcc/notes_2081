6.1 FUNDAMENTALS OF CYBER SECURITY
==================================================================

          . Cyber security means protecting computer systems networks data and
            digital services from unauthorized access misuse damage or disruption
          
          . It protects information systems used by organizations
          
          . Cyber security is important because modern organizations depend on
            digital data and online systems
          
          . MIS stores important information such as customer data employee data
            financial data sales data and business records
          
          . If this information is stolen changed or destroyed the organization
            may suffer financial legal and reputational loss


..................................................................
MAIN GOALS OF CYBER SECURITY
..................................................................

https://delinea.com/blog/what-is-the-cia-triad-key-components-and-examples


          . Confidentiality
          
                    . Information should be accessed only by authorized users
                  
                    . Example
                      Salary records should be viewed only by HR and authorized managers
          
          
          . Integrity
          
                    . Information should be accurate complete and not changed without
                      permission
                  
                    . Example
                      Student marks should not be changed by unauthorized users
                  
          
          . Availability
          
                      . Information and systems should be available when needed
                    
                      . Example
                        Online banking should be available to customers when they need it


..................................................................
CIA TRIAD
..................................................................

              . Confidentiality
                      +
              . Integrity
                      +
              . Availability
                      =
              . Secure Information System

6.2 TYPES OF CYBER THREATS AND ATTACKS
==================================================================

. A cyber threat is a possible danger to information systems

. A cyber attack is an actual attempt to damage steal or misuse data or
  system resources












==================================================================
COMMON CYBER THREATS
==================================================================


..................................................................
1  MALWARE
..................................................................

. Malware means malicious software

. It is designed to damage steal or misuse computer systems

. Examples include virus worm ransomware spyware and trojan


Example

      . An employee downloads an infected file from email
      
      . Malware enters the office computer
      
      . Important files become corrupted or locked
      

..................................................................
2  PHISHING
..................................................................

. Phishing is a fake message designed to trick users

. It may look like a message from bank office college or service provider

. The aim is to steal password OTP or personal information


Example

        . A user receives a fake email saying update your account password
        
        . The user clicks the fake link and enters login details
        
        . The attacker misuses the account


..................................................................
3  RANSOMWARE
..................................................................

. Ransomware locks or encrypts files and demands payment

. It can stop business operations

. It is dangerous for hospitals banks colleges and companies


Example

          . A hospital cannot access patient records because files are locked
          
          . This affects service delivery and patient care


..................................................................
4  PASSWORD ATTACK
..................................................................

. Password attack means trying to access an account by guessing or
  stealing passwords

. Weak passwords increase risk


Weak password example

          . admin123
          
          . password123
          
          . 12345678


Better password practice

        . Use long password
        
        . Use mixed characters
        
        . Use two factor authentication
        
        . Do not reuse same password everywhere
        

..................................................................
5  INSIDER THREAT
..................................................................

. Insider threat comes from people inside the organization

. It may be intentional or accidental

. Employees contractors or partners may misuse access


Example

          . A staff member copies customer records without permission
          
          . A careless employee shares login password with others
          

..................................................................
6  DENIAL OF SERVICE ATTACK
..................................................................

. A denial of service attack makes a system unavailable to users

. The system may become slow or stop responding

. It affects availability


Example

          . An online shopping website becomes unavailable during a sales campaign
          
          . Customers cannot place orders


..................................................................
7  DATA BREACH
..................................................................

. Data breach means unauthorized access to confidential data

. It may expose customer information financial records or business secrets


Example

          . Customer name phone number email and payment data are leaked
          
          . The organization loses trust and may face legal consequences

6.4 PRIVACY AND DATA PROTECTION
==================================================================

. Privacy means the right of individuals to control their personal
  information

. Data protection means protecting personal data from misuse loss theft
  or unauthorized access

. Organizations must collect only necessary data

. They must use data only for the declared purpose

. They must store data securely

. They must not share personal data without permission


==================================================================
PERSONAL DATA EXAMPLES
==================================================================
          
          . Name
          
          . Address
          
          . Phone number
          
          . Email
          
          . Citizenship number
          
          . Bank account details
          
          . Health records
          
          . Academic records
          
          . Location data
          
          . Online activity
          
          . Biometric data


==================================================================
GOOD DATA PROTECTION PRACTICES
==================================================================

          . Collect minimum required data
          
          . Inform users why data is collected
          
          . Take user consent
          
          . Store data securely
          
          . Limit access based on role
          
          . Delete unnecessary data
          
          . Use encryption for sensitive data
          
          . Maintain backup
          
          . Monitor access logs
          
          . Follow legal requirements


..................................................................
EXAMPLE
..................................................................

. A college collects student phone number and email for academic notices

. It should not sell or share that data with advertisers

. Using student data for unrelated marketing without permission is
  unethical

6.7 SECURITY AUDIT PROCESSES AND METHODOLOGIES
==================================================================

. Security audit is a systematic review of information system security

. It checks whether systems data users and procedures are properly
  protected

. It helps identify weaknesses risks and compliance gaps


==================================================================
OBJECTIVES OF SECURITY AUDIT
==================================================================

                        . Identify security weaknesses
                        
                        . Check access control
                        
                        . Review user activities
                        
                        . Verify backup process
                        
                        . Check policy compliance
                        
                        . Review system logs
                        
                        . Evaluate risk
                        
                        . Recommend improvements
                        
                        . Improve accountability
                        

==================================================================
SECURITY AUDIT PROCESS
==================================================================
        
        . Planning
        
          . Define audit scope objective and system to be reviewed
        
        
        . Information collection
        
          . Collect documents policies system details and user access records
        
        
        . Risk assessment
        
          . Identify possible risks and their impact
        
        
        . Control evaluation
        
          . Check whether security controls are working properly
        
        
        . Testing and review
        
          . Review logs permissions backups updates and procedures
        
        
        . Reporting
        
          . Prepare audit findings risk level and recommendations
        
        
        . Follow up
        
          . Check whether corrective actions are implemented


..................................................................
SECURITY AUDIT FLOW
..................................................................
                
                . Audit Planning
                        ↓
                . Data Collection
                        ↓
                . Risk Assessment
                        ↓
                . Control Testing
                        ↓
                . Audit Report
                        ↓
                . Corrective Action
                        ↓
                . Follow Up

==================================================================
CASE STUDY 1  CUSTOMER DATA MISUSE IN ONLINE STORE
==================================================================

. A growing online store collects customer name phone number address and
  order history

. The marketing team wants to share customer phone numbers with a third
  party advertising company

. Customers were not informed about this use of their data

. The company has no clear privacy policy

            
            ..................................................................
            ETHICAL ISSUES
            ..................................................................
            
            . Customer data may be used without consent
            
            . Customer privacy may be violated
            
            . Trust may be damaged
            
            . Data may be misused by third party
            
            
            ..................................................................
            LEGAL ISSUES
            ..................................................................
            
            . Violation of privacy requirements
            
            . Lack of consent
            
            . Weak data protection practice
            
            . Possible legal complaint from customers
            
            
            ..................................................................
            RECOMMENDED SOLUTIONS
            ..................................................................
            
            . Create clear privacy policy
            
            . Take customer consent
            
            . Share only necessary data if legally allowed
            
            . Use data anonymization where possible
            
            . Limit third party access
            
            . Maintain data sharing agreement
            
            . Train employees about data ethics
