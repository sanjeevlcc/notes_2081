1.1 DEFINITION OF MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEM
==================================================================
          
          . MIS stands for Management Information System
          
          . MIS is an organized system that collects processes stores and
            distributes information to managers
          
          . It helps managers in planning controlling coordinating and making
            decisions
          
          . MIS converts raw business data into meaningful information
          
          . Raw data alone may not be useful for decision making
          
          . After processing data becomes useful information


..................................................................
SIMPLE EXAMPLE
..................................................................
            
            . A shop records daily sales transactions
            
            . Individual sales records are data
            
            . Total daily sales best selling product and remaining stock are
              information
            
            . The manager uses this information to decide
            
                          . Which product should be purchased again
                        
                          . Which product is not selling
                        
                          . How much stock is available
                        
                          . Whether sales are increasing or decreasing

EVOLUTION OF MIS
==================================================================

. Information systems have developed gradually with changes in business
  and technology

            
            ..................................................................
            STAGE 1  MANUAL RECORD KEEPING
            ..................................................................
                      
                      . Information was recorded in registers and paper files
                      
                      . Calculations were performed manually
                      
                      . Searching old information was difficult
                      
                      . Reports required more time
            
            
            ..................................................................
            STAGE 2  ELECTRONIC DATA PROCESSING
            ..................................................................
            
                      . Computers were used for routine calculations
                      
                      . Main focus was payroll accounting and billing
                      
                      . Systems mainly processed transactions
            
            
            ..................................................................
            STAGE 3  MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEM
            ..................................................................
            
                      . Systems started producing reports for managers
                      
                      . Managers received sales inventory and performance reports
                      
                      . Information was used for planning and control
                      
            
            ..................................................................
            STAGE 4  DECISION SUPPORT SYSTEM
            ..................................................................
            
                    . Systems started supporting complex and semi structured decisions
                    
                    . Managers could compare alternatives and perform what if analysis
                    
                    
            ..................................................................
            STAGE 5  STRATEGIC AND INTELLIGENT SYSTEMS
            ..................................................................
                  
                  . Systems started using business intelligence artificial intelligence
                    cloud computing big data and analytics
                  
                  . Information became a strategic business resource




..................................................................
EVOLUTION SUMMARY
..................................................................

                . Paper Records
                        ↓
                . Transaction Processing
                        ↓
                . Management Reporting
                        ↓
                . Decision Support
                        ↓
                . Executive Information
                        ↓
                . Business Intelligence and AI

1.2 TYPES OF INFORMATION SYSTEMS
==================================================================

          
          ==================================================================
          A  TRANSACTION PROCESSING SYSTEM
          ==================================================================
          
          . TPS stands for Transaction Processing System
          
          . TPS records daily routine business transactions
          
          . It works at the operational level
          
          . It handles large numbers of repeated transactions
          
          . TPS information is detailed and current
          
          
          ..................................................................
          EXAMPLES OF TPS
          ..................................................................
          
                . Sales billing system
                
                . ATM transaction system
                
                . Payroll system
                
                . Hotel reservation system
                
                . Online order processing system
                
                . College fee collection system
                
                . Inventory transaction system
                
          
          ..................................................................
          TPS EXAMPLE
          ..................................................................
          
                  . A supermarket customer purchases five products
                  
                  . The billing system
                  
                    . Reads product codes
                  
                    . Calculates total price
                  
                    . Updates inventory
                  
                    . Prints the bill
                  
                    . Stores the transaction
                  
                    . All these activities are performed by TPS
          
          
          ==================================================================
          B  MANAGEMENT INFORMATION SYSTEM
          ==================================================================
          
          . MIS uses transaction data to prepare summarized reports
          
          . MIS mainly supports middle level managers
          
          . It supports structured and routine decisions
          
          . MIS reports may be daily weekly monthly or yearly
          
          
          ..................................................................
          EXAMPLES OF MIS REPORTS
          ..................................................................
          
                  . Monthly sales report
                  
                  . Department wise expense report
                  
                  . Inventory status report
                  
                  . Employee attendance report
                  
                  . Product performance report
                  
                  . Student result summary
          
          
          ..................................................................
          EXAMPLE
          ..................................................................
          
          . TPS records every individual sale
          
          . MIS combines those sales and produces a monthly report


          
                  Region        Monthly Sales        Target        Achievement
                  ..................................................................      
                  Butwal          1200000            1000000           120 percent
                  
                  Pokhara          850000             900000            94 percent
                  
                  Kathmandu       1600000            1500000           107 percent
          
          
          . Management can identify that Pokhara did not achieve its target
          
          . Managers can investigate product price promotion and sales staff
            performance
          
          
          ==================================================================
          C  DECISION SUPPORT SYSTEM
          ==================================================================
          
          . DSS stands for Decision Support System
          
          . DSS supports managers in semi structured and non routine decisions
          
          . It uses data models analysis and comparison
          
          . DSS helps managers evaluate different alternatives
          
          . DSS does not replace managers
          
          . It improves the decision making process
          
          
          ..................................................................
          EXAMPLES OF DSS
          ..................................................................
          
                  . Loan approval analysis
                  
                  . Investment selection
                  
                  . Production planning
                  
                  . Sales forecasting
                  
                  . Location selection
                  
                  . Budget allocation
                  
                  . What if analysis
          
          
          ..................................................................
          WHAT IF EXAMPLE
          ..................................................................
          
          . A business wants to know the effect of a price reduction
          
                    Current price                    NPR 1000
                    
                    Current monthly sales            500 units
                    
                    Expected sales after discount    650 units
                    
                    New price                        NPR 900
          
          
          Current revenue
          
          . 1000 multiplied by 500 equals NPR 500000
          
          
          Expected revenue after discount
          
          . 900 multiplied by 650 equals NPR 585000
          
          
          . DSS shows that the discount may increase total revenue by NPR 85000





          
          ==================================================================
          D  EXECUTIVE SUPPORT SYSTEM
          ==================================================================
          
          . ESS stands for Executive Support System
          
          . ESS supports senior executives and top level management
          
          . It provides highly summarized strategic information
          
          . It uses dashboards charts trends and external information
          
          . It supports long term and unstructured decisions
          
          
          ..................................................................
          EXAMPLES OF ESS INFORMATION
          ..................................................................
                  
                  . Overall company profit
                  
                  . Market share
                  
                  . Industry growth
                  
                  . Competitor performance
                  
                  . Regional business performance
                  
                  . Long term sales trend
                  
                  . Economic and political conditions
          
          
          ..................................................................
          EXAMPLE
          ..................................................................
                
                . The chief executive views a dashboard containing
                
                  . Total annual revenue
                
                  . Profit growth
                
                  . Market share
                
                  . Customer satisfaction
                
                  . Regional performance
                
                  . Competitor activity
                
                . The executive uses the dashboard to decide whether the company should
                  enter a new market





          ==================================================================
          COMPARISON OF TPS MIS DSS AND ESS
          ==================================================================
          
          System     Management Level     Main Purpose                Example
          
          TPS        Operational          Record transactions         Billing
          
          MIS        Middle               Generate reports            Sales report
          
          DSS        Middle and Senior    Analyze alternatives        Forecasting
          
          ESS        Top                  Strategic decisions         Executive dashboard

CASE  DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION OF HIMALAYAN RETAIL STORE
--------------------------------------------------------------
          . Himalayan Retail Store operates five branches in Nepal
          
          . Each branch currently records sales and inventory separately
          
          . Managers receive sales reports after several days
          
          . Some branches have excess stock while others face shortages
          
          . Customer complaints are recorded manually
          
          . The company wants to launch online shopping and home delivery
          
          . Management also wants a dashboard for sales profit stock and customer satisfaction

                                        
                                        ..................................................................
                                        PRESENT PROBLEMS
                                        ..................................................................
                                        
                                                  . Delayed information
                                                  
                                                  . Duplicate data entry
                                                  
                                                  . Inventory shortage
                                                  
                                                  . Excess inventory
                                                  
                                                  . Weak customer service
                                                  
                                                  . No online sales platform
                                                  
                                                  . Difficult branch comparison
                                                  
                                                  . Slow managerial decisions
                                        
                                        
                                        ..................................................................
                                        PROPOSED INFORMATION SYSTEMS
                                        ..................................................................
                                        
                                        TPS
                                        
                                        . Record daily sales payments returns and stock movements
                                        
                                        
                                        MIS
                                        
                                        . Generate branch wise sales stock profit and expense reports
                                        
                                        
                                        DSS
                                        
                                        . Forecast demand and recommend stock quantities
                                        
                                        
                                        ESS
                                        
                                        . Present total revenue profit market growth and branch performance
                                        
                                        
                                        E Commerce System
                                        
                                        . Allow customers to browse order pay and track delivery
                                        
                                        
                                        CRM
                                        
                                        . Store customer history complaints preferences and feedback
                                        
                                        
                                        ..................................................................
                                        SAMPLE BRANCH DATA
                                        ..................................................................
                                        
                                                  Branch        Monthly Sales        Stock Value        Complaints
                                                  
                                                  Butwal          1800000              900000               35
                                                  
                                                  Pokhara         1500000             1200000               18
                                                  
                                                  Kathmandu       2500000             1000000               42
                                                  
                                                  Bhairahawa      1200000              700000               25
                                                  
                                                  Chitwan         1600000             1100000               20
                                                  
                                        
                                        ..................................................................
                                        SIMPLE ANALYSIS
                                        ..................................................................
                                        
                                                  . Kathmandu has the highest monthly sales
                                                  
                                                  . Kathmandu also has the highest number of complaints
                                                  
                                                  . Pokhara has relatively high stock compared with sales
                                                  
                                                  . Bhairahawa has the lowest sales
                                                  
                                                  . Management should study customer complaints in Kathmandu
                                                  
                                                  . Management should review stock allocation in Pokhara
                                                  
                                                  . DSS can forecast branch demand
                                                  
                                                  . ESS can show overall strategic performance
