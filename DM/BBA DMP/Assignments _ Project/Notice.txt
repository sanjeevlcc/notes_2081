NOTICE : DIGITAL MARKETING PRACTICUM – GROUP PROJECT
............................................................................

All students are informed that a practical GROUP PROJECT will be conducted
under the Digital Marketing Practicum.

Group Formation
............................................................
• 4 students per group (compulsory)

Project Tasks (Short)
............................................................
• Select a small business idea / brand topic
• Branding ............ Business name, slogan, logo, color theme
• Website Creation ............ Home, About, Products/Services, Contact
• Website Running ............ Host / run the website and ensure it works properly
• Contact Setup ............ Add email link, phone number, contact form, location/map
• Content Creation ............ Posts/blogs/posters/videos (minimum content required)
• Social Media ............ Create/manage page(s) and publish posts
• Basic SEO ............ Keywords, title, meta description, headings, image alt text
• Google Analytics ............ Link website with Google Analytics and track visitors
• Report ............ Short analytics/report + screenshots as evidence

Submission Requirements
............................................................
• Website link
• Logo/branding files
• Content samples
• Social media link(s)
• Google Analytics screenshot/report

Note
............................................................
• Equal contribution from all group members is compulsory.
• Timely completion and submission of all assigned work is mandatory.
