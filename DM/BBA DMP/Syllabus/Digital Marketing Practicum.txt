List of the Practicals
............................................................................

Faculty members are empowered to adapt and modify course content to align with
market needs and contextual demands, provided that the core objectives and
practical aspects of the course remain intact and relevant.

The suggested course contents are as follows:

............................................................................
Module 1 : Introduction to Digital Marketing (1 Hour)
............................................................................
• Overview of digital marketing and its importance
• Key channels in digital marketing ............ SEO, SEM, Social Media,
  Email Marketing, Content Marketing, etc.
• Establishing a personal or small business brand online

............................................................................
Module 2 : Website Development and Optimization (3 Hours)
............................................................................
• Basics of website creation ............ WordPress, Wix, etc.
• User Experience (UX) and User Interface (UI)
• Introduction to SEO ............ On-page and Off-page

............................................................................
Module 3 : Content Creation and Management (3 Hours)
............................................................................
• Creating engaging content ............ Blogs, Videos, Infographics
• Overview of Content Management Systems (CMS)
• Content calendar and scheduling

............................................................................
Module 4 : Social Media Marketing (2 Hours)
............................................................................
• Overview of platforms ............ Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn
• Developing a social media strategy
• Crafting and managing social media campaigns

............................................................................
Module 5 : Search Engine Marketing (2 Hours)
............................................................................
• Overview of Google Ads and PPC campaigns
• Keyword research and ad creation
• Measuring and optimizing ad performance

............................................................................
Module 6 : Email Marketing (1 Hour)
............................................................................
• Building and segmenting email lists
• Writing effective email campaigns
• Email tools ............ MailChimp, Constant Contact

............................................................................
Module 7 : Analytics and Reporting (2 Hours)
............................................................................
• Introduction to Google Analytics
• Tracking and interpreting key metrics
• Creating actionable reports

............................................................................
Module 8 : Practical Project and Presentation (2 Hours)
............................................................................
• Completion of a practical digital marketing project
• Project presentation, feedback, and discussion

............................................................................
Note
............................................................................
Figures in parentheses indicate approximate teaching hours for each module.
