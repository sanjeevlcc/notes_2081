SECTION C : LONG NUMERICAL + ANALYTICAL + FIGURE QUESTIONS (15)
(APPLY + ANALYZE + EVALUATE + CREATE)
          ============================================================
          
          1. Mid-Square Method
          ------------------------------------------------------------
          
          Generate 5 random numbers using Mid-Square method.
          Initial seed = 5735
          
          
          2. Residue Method
          ------------------------------------------------------------
          
          Generate 8 random numbers using:
          
          Xi+1 = (5Xi + 3) mod 16
          
          Initial seed X0 = 7
          
          
          3. Monte Carlo Simulation
          ------------------------------------------------------------
          
          A shop has daily demand probability:
          
          Demand     Probability
          0          0.10
          1          0.20
          2          0.30
          3          0.25
          4          0.15
          
          Use random numbers:
          15, 37, 82, 56, 91, 22
          
          Simulate demand for 6 days.
          
          
          4. Chi-Square Test
          ------------------------------------------------------------
          
          Given random numbers:
          
          12, 25, 44, 62, 78, 80, 95, 50, 40, 10
          
          Test uniformity using Chi-square test.
          
          
          5. Queuing Numerical
          ------------------------------------------------------------
          
          Customers arrive at a bank at rate λ = 10/hour.
          Service rate μ = 15/hour.
          
          Calculate:
          . Utilization factor
          . Average number in system
          . Average waiting time
          
          
          6. Single Server Queue
          ------------------------------------------------------------
          
          Arrival rate = 12 customers/hour
          Service rate = 18 customers/hour
          
          Calculate:
          . Average queue length
          . Average waiting time
          
          
          7. Markov Chain
          ------------------------------------------------------------
          
          Transition Matrix:
          
          P = [0.7 0.3]
              [0.4 0.6]
          
          Find probability after 2 transitions.
          
          
          8. Regression Analysis
          ------------------------------------------------------------
          
          Given data:
          
          X = 1, 2, 3, 4, 5
          Y = 2, 4, 5, 4, 5
          
          Find regression line.
          
          
          9. Traffic Simulation Model
          ------------------------------------------------------------
          
          Design a simulation model for traffic management at a busy
          city intersection considering:
          
          . Vehicle flow
          . Traffic lights
          . Pedestrians
          . Weather
          
          Draw conceptual diagram.
          
          
          10. Serial Chase Problem
          ------------------------------------------------------------
          
          A car moves at 60 km/hr.
          A bike starts 20 km behind at 80 km/hr.
          
          Model and analyze chase problem.
          
          
          11. Feedback System
          ------------------------------------------------------------
          
          Draw and explain a feedback control system using block diagram.
          
          
          12. Software Process Simulation
          ------------------------------------------------------------
          
          A software company has:
          . 5 developers
          . 2 testers
          . 3 modules
          
          Model software development process using waterfall model.
          
          
          13. ANOVA
          ------------------------------------------------------------
          
          Three machine outputs are:
          
          A: 20, 22, 23
          B: 25, 27, 29
          C: 18, 19, 21
          
          Perform ANOVA test.
          
          
          14. Hospital Queue Simulation
          ------------------------------------------------------------
          
          Design a queuing model for hospital registration system.
          
          Draw figure and explain:
          . Arrival
          . Queue
          . Service
          . Exit
          
          
          15. Agent-Based Simulation
          ------------------------------------------------------------
          
          Design an agent-based simulation for online shopping system.
          
          Consider:
          . Customer agents
          . Seller agents
          . Payment agents
          . Delivery agents

          Draw architecture diagram and explain.
