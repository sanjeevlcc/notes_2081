


Q1) If the arrival rate is λ = 5 customers/hour and service rate is μ = 10 customers/hour,
    find the traffic intensity ρ.
Solution:
ρ = λ / μ = 5 / 10 = 0.5
------------------------------------------------------------

Q2) A system has λ = 2 customers/hour and μ = 8 customers/hour.
    Find Ls (average number of customers in system).
Solution:
Ls = λ / (μ − λ) = 2 / (8 − 2) = 2 / 6 = 0.33 customers
------------------------------------------------------------

Q3) A queue has λ = 4 customers/hour and μ = 12 customers/hour.
    Find Lq (average number in queue).
Solution:
Lq = λ² / [ μ (μ − λ) ]
Lq = 4² / [ 12 × (12 − 4) ] = 16 / (12 × 8) = 16 / 96 = 0.1667 customers
------------------------------------------------------------

Q1) In a small service center, customers arrive at an average rate λ = 4 per hour.
    The service rate is μ = 10 per hour. Assume an M/M/1 queue.
    Find:
    (a) Traffic intensity ρ
    (b) Average number of customers in the system Ls
    (c) Average number of customers in the queue Lq

Solution 1:
- Formula: ρ = λ / μ
- ρ = 4 / 10 = 0.4

- Formula (M/M/1): Ls = λ / (μ − λ)
- Ls = 4 / (10 − 4) = 4 / 6 = 0.6667 ≈ 0.67 customers

- Formula (M/M/1): Lq = λ² / [ μ (μ − λ) ]
- Lq = 4² / [ 10 × (10 − 4) ] = 16 / (10 × 6) = 16 / 60 = 0.2667 ≈ 0.27 customers
- Answer: ρ = 0.4, Ls ≈ 0.67, Lq ≈ 0.27

Q2) A barber shop has arrivals at rate λ = 3 customers/hour.
    The service rate is μ = 5 customers/hour. Assume M/M/1.
    Find:
    (a) ρ
    (b) Average time a customer spends in the system Ws
    (c) Average waiting time in the queue Wq

Solution 2:
- ρ = λ / μ = 3 / 5 = 0.6

- Formula (M/M/1): Ws = 1 / (μ − λ)
- Ws = 1 / (5 − 3) = 1 / 2 = 0.5 hours = 30 minutes

- Formula (M/M/1): Wq = λ / [ μ (μ − λ) ]
- Wq = 3 / [ 5 × (5 − 3) ] = 3 / (5 × 2) = 3 / 10 = 0.3 hours = 18 minutes

- Answer: ρ = 0.6, Ws = 0.5 hr (30 min), Wq = 0.3 hr (18 min)

Q3) A photocopy center receives λ = 6 jobs/hour.
    The machine serves at μ = 8 jobs/hour (M/M/1).
    Find:
    (a) ρ
    (b) Ls (average number in the system)
    (c) Lq (average queue length)
    (d) Ws (average time in system)
    (e) Wq (average waiting time in queue)

Solution 3:
- ρ = λ / μ = 6 / 8 = 0.75

- Ls = λ / (μ − λ) = 6 / (8 − 6) = 6 / 2 = 3 customers

- Lq = λ² / [ μ (μ − λ) ]
- Lq = 6² / [ 8 × (8 − 6) ] = 36 / (8 × 2) = 36 / 16 = 2.25 customers

- Ws = 1 / (μ − λ) = 1 / (8 − 6) = 1 / 2 = 0.5 hours = 30 minutes

- Wq = λ / [ μ (μ − λ) ]
- Wq = 6 / [ 8 × (8 − 6) ] = 6 / (8 × 2) = 6 / 16 = 0.375 hours = 22.5 minutes

- Answer:
  ρ = 0.75, Ls = 3, Lq = 2.25, Ws = 0.5 hr, Wq = 0.375 hr

Q4) A clinic receives λ = 10 patients/hour.
    The doctor can treat patients at μ = 12 patients/hour (M/M/1).
    Find:
    (a) ρ
    (b) Ls
    (c) Probability that no patients are in the system P0

Solution 4:
- ρ = λ / μ = 10 / 12 = 0.8333 ≈ 0.83

- Ls = λ / (μ − λ) = 10 / (12 − 10) = 10 / 2 = 5 patients

- In M/M/1: P0 = 1 − ρ
- P0 = 1 − 0.8333 = 0.1667 ≈ 0.17

- Answer: ρ ≈ 0.83, Ls = 5, P0 ≈ 0.17

Q5) A self-service kiosk in a mall serves customers with the following rates:
    Arrival rate λ = 18 customers/hour.
    Service rate μ = 25 customers/hour (M/M/1).
    Find:
    (a) ρ
    (b) Ls
    (c) Lq
    (d) Ws
    (e) Wq

Solution 5:
- ρ = λ / μ = 18 / 25 = 0.72

- Ls = λ / (μ − λ) = 18 / (25 − 18) = 18 / 7 ≈ 2.5714 customers

- Lq = λ² / [ μ (μ − λ) ]
- Lq = 18² / [ 25 × (25 − 18) ] = 324 / [ 25 × 7 ] = 324 / 175 ≈ 1.8514 customers

- Ws = 1 / (μ − λ) = 1 / (25 − 18) = 1 / 7 ≈ 0.1429 hours
  (≈ 0.1429 × 60 ≈ 8.57 minutes)

- Wq = λ / [ μ (μ − λ) ]
- Wq = 18 / [ 25 × 7 ] = 18 / 175 ≈ 0.1029 hours
  (≈ 0.1029 × 60 ≈ 6.17 minutes)

- Answer:
  ρ = 0.72
  Ls ≈ 2.57 customers
  Lq ≈ 1.85 customers
  Ws ≈ 0.143 hr (≈ 8.6 min)
  Wq ≈ 0.103 hr (≈ 6.2 min)
